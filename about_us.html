About the wedsite

Welcome to the Tale Time Antiquities Museum website of the Myanmar Antiquities Museum, your first port of call for discovering the rich and varied history of Myanmar. The outstanding antiques and historical riches that represent the nation's ancient civilizations and cultural past are the focus of our museum, which is committed to its preservation and exhibition. We provide guests with a thorough overview of our vast collections via our online platform, which includes fine artifacts, comprehensive displays, and perceptive research. Learn the interesting histories that have molded Myanmar into the dynamic country it is now, as well as the tales behind our objects and the most recent exhibitions and events. Come along on this historical voyage with us and get lost in Myanmar's vibrant culture.

Mission

Our mission is to preserve, protect, and display an extensive collection of antiquities so that both the general public and academics can view them. Our mission is to deeply engage, educate, and inspire visitors while fostering a comprehensive understanding of the artistic, cultural, and historical accomplishments of ancient societies via exciting exhibitions, educational programs, and state-of-the-art research. We are dedicated to supporting the continued study and appreciation of antiquities, promoting cultural heritage stewardship, and creating a sense of connection to the past.

Vission

To become a globally recognized hub for the conservation and commemoration of human history, where the historical past is brought to life. Our goal is to create a profound understanding of our common history and the enduring legacy of antiquity by inspiring and educating people of all ages about the diverse range of cultures and civilizations that have shaped our world.
